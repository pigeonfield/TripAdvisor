A star rating system built with jQuery

The plugin has a lots of settings, allowing the developer to build his own custom rating system, based on his products and features.

A demo is included with the download package.

System requirements
  • JavaScript enabled on client side
  • jQuery
New in jQuery Raty 2.6.0:
